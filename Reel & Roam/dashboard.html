Paris, France

Overview

Known as the "City of Light," Paris is famous for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city is home to iconic landmarks including the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Must-See Attractions

Tokyo, Japan

Overview

Tokyo is a bustling metropolis blending the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. It is known for its cuisine, technology, and vibrant culture.

Must-See Attractions
Senso-ji Temple

Ancient Buddhist temple

Tokyo Skytree

Tallest tower in Japan

Shibuya Crossing

World's busiest intersection

Tsukiji Outer Market

Famous fish market

Meiji Shrine

Peaceful shrine in forest

Local Cuisine
  • Sushi and sashimi
  • Ramen noodles
  • Tempura (battered seafood/vegetables)
  • Takoyaki (octopus balls)
  • Matcha green tea desserts
Budget Tips
  • Use JR Pass for train travel
  • Stay in capsule hotels or hostels
  • Eat at convenience stores (konbini)
  • Visit during cherry blossom season for free views
  • Use free observation decks instead of paid ones

Bali, Indonesia

Overview

Bali is a tropical paradise known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs. It is a popular destination for relaxation and adventure.

Must-See Attractions
Tanah Lot Temple

Sea temple on rock formation

Tegalalang Rice Terraces

Stunning terraced rice fields

Ubud Monkey Forest

Sacred monkey sanctuary

Mount Batur

Active volcano with sunrise views

Nusa Penida

Island paradise with crystal waters

Local Cuisine
  • Nasi Goreng (fried rice)
  • Mie Goreng (fried noodles)
  • Satay (grilled meat skewers)
  • Gado-gado (vegetable salad)
  • Babi Guling (suckling pig)
Budget Tips
  • Stay in guesthouses instead of resorts
  • Eat at local warungs (small restaurants)
  • Rent a scooter for transportation
  • Visit during shoulder season (Apr-Jun, Sep-Nov)
  • Bargain at markets but be respectful

New York City, USA

Overview

The Big Apple is a global center of culture, finance, and entertainment. Known for its iconic skyline, diverse neighborhoods, and world-class museums and restaurants.

Must-See Attractions
Statue of Liberty

Iconic symbol of freedom

Central Park

Urban oasis in the city

Times Square

Bright lights and energy

Empire State Building

Art deco skyscraper

Broadway

World-famous theater district

Local Cuisine
  • New York pizza (thin crust)
  • Hot dogs from street vendors
  • Bagels with cream cheese
  • Pastrami sandwiches
  • Cheesecake from Junior's
Budget Tips
  • Use the subway for transportation
  • Visit museums on free days
  • Eat at food trucks and delis
  • Stay in Brooklyn or Queens for cheaper accommodation
  • Walk across Brooklyn Bridge for free views

London, UK

Overview

London is a historic city that blends centuries of tradition with modern innovation. Home to royal palaces, world-class museums, and diverse neighborhoods.

Must-See Attractions
Big Ben & Parliament

Iconic clock tower

Buckingham Palace

Royal residence

Tower of London

Historic castle

British Museum

World artifacts collection

London Eye

Giant observation wheel

Local Cuisine
  • Fish and chips
  • Full English breakfast
  • Sunday roast with Yorkshire pudding
  • Afternoon tea with scones
  • Pie and mash
Budget Tips
  • Use Oyster card for public transport
  • Visit free museums (British Museum, National Gallery)
  • Eat at markets like Borough Market
  • Stay in Zone 2-3 for cheaper accommodation
  • Walk along the Thames for free views

Sydney, Australia

Overview

Sydney is Australia's largest city, known for its stunning harbor, iconic Opera House, beautiful beaches, and vibrant culture. A perfect blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty.

Must-See Attractions
Sydney Opera House

Architectural masterpiece

Sydney Harbour Bridge

Iconic bridge

Bondi Beach

Famous surf beach

Royal Botanic Garden

Beautiful gardens

Darling Harbour

Entertainment precinct

Local Cuisine
  • Meat pies and sausage rolls
  • Fish and chips
  • Pavlova dessert
  • Lamington cakes
  • Fresh seafood from the harbor
Budget Tips
  • Use Opal card for public transport
  • Visit during shoulder season (Mar-May, Sep-Nov)
  • Eat at food courts and markets
  • Stay in suburbs like Surry Hills or Newtown
  • Walk the Bondi to Coogee coastal walk (free)
